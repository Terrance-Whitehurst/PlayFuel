## Section A: User-Facing Disclaimer (Verbatim from §18)

Display this disclaimer in the app. Suggested locations: onboarding flow, settings screen, plan view footer.

> This app provides general tournament preparation guidance. It is not medical advice, nutrition therapy, or a substitute for a coach, physician, athletic trainer, or registered dietitian. For injuries, illness, heat symptoms, allergies, eating disorders, or medical conditions, consult a qualified professional.

## Section B: Hard-Coded Emergency Guidance (Verbatim from §18)

This text must be hard-coded in the app and backend. It must **not** be generated by the LLM. Display it whenever heat, illness, or safety-related keywords appear in context, and make it accessible in the plan UI.

> If your player feels faint or confused, has chest pain, stops sweating in extreme heat, has severe cramps, vomits repeatedly, or shows signs of heat illness: stop play and seek medical help. Call 911 (or your local emergency number) in an emergency.

## Section C: Phrases the App and LLM Must NEVER Produce

The following phrases, or any semantically equivalent statements, are **prohibited** in all app text, LLM output, push notifications, and UI copy.

### Prohibited Phrases (from §28)

| Prohibited | Why |
|---|---|
| "This will prevent cramps." | Medical/causal claim |
| "This will prevent heat illness." | Medical/causal claim |
| "This is safe for every player." | Blanket safety claim |
| "This injury is minor." | Medical diagnosis |
| "Keep playing through pain." | Unsafe instruction |
| "This guarantees better performance." | Performance guarantee |
| "This food guarantees better performance." | Performance guarantee |
| "This solves injury risk." | Medical/safety claim |

### Additional Prohibited Patterns

These are not verbatim from §28 but are implied by §8.7 (LLM must not invent) and §16 (food guidance):

| Prohibited | Why |
|---|---|
| Invented restaurant names or addresses | LLM must not fabricate locations |
| Invented menu items or prices | LLM must not fabricate menu data |
| Specific hydration quantities as medical prescription | Not within app's scope |
| Weather facts that differ from the structured weather input | LLM must not override structured data |
| Tournament rules interpretations | Not within app's scope |
| Claims that a specific food "prevents" any outcome | Causal medical/nutrition claim |

## Section D: Safer Language (Required Substitutions)

When describing recommendations, guidance, or suggestions, use this safer language pattern.

| Instead of | Use |
|---|---|
| "Will prevent…" | "Consider…" |
| "Guarantees…" | "Generally…" |
| "You must…" | "A common option is…" |
| "This is safe for your player" | "If tolerated…" |
| "Your doctor recommends…" | "If your coach/doctor has advised…" |
| Any medical instruction | "Seek medical attention if…" |
| "This is the right amount to drink" | "Staying hydrated during hot conditions is generally important. Adjust based on how your player feels." |

## Section E: LLM System Prompt Requirements (from §8.7)

Every LLM call for plan explanation must include these instructions in the system prompt:

```
You are a junior tennis tournament assistant.

You will receive a structured plan generated by our rules engine.

Your job:
- Explain the plan clearly to a parent.
- Use a calm, practical tone.
- Do not invent restaurants, menu items, weather facts, match rules, or medical advice.
- Do not change the structured timing.
- Mention that injury or illness concerns should be handled by a qualified professional.
- Keep the plan concise enough to read during a tournament.

Structured plan:
{{PLAN_JSON}}
```

## Section F: Privacy Safety for Minors (from §27)

| Constraint | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Child data | Parent account owns all data. No child-owned accounts in MVP. |
| Age collection | Use age range or birth year only. Do not collect exact birthdate. |
| Sensitive fields | Injury notes and dietary notes are optional, never required. |
| Data use | No data selling. No targeted advertising. |
| Player profiles | No public player profiles. |
| Location | Store tournament venue location only. Do not store live player location history. |
| COPPA | If any user under 13 may use the app, legal review required before launch (see FTC source §27). |
| Deletion | Data deletion must be implemented before any public release. |
